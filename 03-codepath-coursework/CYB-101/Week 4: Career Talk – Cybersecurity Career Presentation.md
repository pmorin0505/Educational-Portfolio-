# Week 4: Career Talk – Cybersecurity Career Presentation

## Overview

This week featured a career presentation session where industry mentors shared insights on cybersecurity, career development, and professional growth in the technology field.

The session focused on real-world expectations in cybersecurity roles and advice for students entering the industry.

---

## Topics Covered

- Career paths in cybersecurity and IT
- Entry-level expectations in the industry
- Importance of hands-on experience and certifications
- Communication and professionalism in technical roles
- Growth mindset and continuous learning in tech careers

---

## Key Takeaways

- Technical skills alone are not enough; communication and adaptability are equally important
- Building experience early through labs, CTFs, and projects strengthens career readiness
- Cybersecurity roles often require constant learning due to evolving threats
- Networking and mentorship play a major role in career development

---

## Reflection

This session helped connect classroom learning to real industry expectations. It provided clarity on how skills being developed in coursework and labs translate into cybersecurity careers.

It also reinforced the importance of continuing to build technical experience through projects, certifications, and practical exercises.
